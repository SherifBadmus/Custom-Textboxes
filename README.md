## Overview

The **Custom Textboxes** mod for Geometry Dash allows players to create and customize textboxes in their levels. This mod enhances the gameplay experience by providing a wide range of options for text display, making it easier to convey messages, instructions, or just add a personal touch to your levels.

## Features

- **Highly Customizable**: Adjust font size, color, and style to fit your level's theme.
- **Dynamic Positioning**: Place textboxes anywhere on the screen.
- **Multiple Text Options**: Add different text styles and effects.
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Easy to use, even for beginners.
- **Support for Multiple Languages**: Create textboxes in various languages.
- **Lightweight**: Minimal impact on game performance.

## Installation

To get started, download the latest version of the mod from the [Releases](https://github.com/SherifBadmus/Custom-Textboxes/releases) section. Once downloaded, execute the file to install the mod.

## Usage

1. **Open Geometry Dash**.
2. **Create or edit a level**.
3. **Access the Custom Textboxes menu** from the mod interface.
4. **Customize your text**: Choose your font, size, color, and position.
5. **Save your changes** and enjoy your newly enhanced level!

## Customization Options

### Fonts

Choose from a variety of fonts to match your level's style. You can upload custom fonts as well.

### Colors

Set the text color using RGB values or select from a color wheel. You can also add gradients for a more dynamic look.

### Sizes

Adjust the font size to ensure your text is readable and fits well within your level's design.

### Effects

Add effects like shadows, outlines, or glows to make your text stand out.

### Positioning

Drag and drop your textboxes anywhere on the screen. Use the grid for precise placement.
